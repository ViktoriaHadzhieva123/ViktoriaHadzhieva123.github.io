Introduction

Italy (the Italian Republic) is a unitary parliamentary republic in Europe. It is located at the heart of the Mediterranean Sea. Italy shares open borders with France, Switzerland, Austria, Slovenia, San Marino and Vatican City. It does not have a largely temperature seasonal differentiation and Mediterrean climate. Its population is of around 61 million inhabitants.
